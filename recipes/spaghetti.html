Spaghetti

Description

This is a simple recipe that can easily be adjust to accommodate taste preference. This recipe has an overall sweet taste.

  • noodles
  • hamburger
  • tomato sauce
  • italian seasoning
  • garlic salt
  • sugar
  1. Brown the hamburger
  2. Add 1 can of tomato sauce for every pound of hamburger used
  3. Start boiling noodles
  4. Add italian seasoning, garlic salt, and sugar to sauce
  5. Combine noodles and sauce on a plate or bowl and enjoy!
